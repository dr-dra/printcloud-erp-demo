# Product Guidelines - PrintCloud

## Visual Aesthetic & UX
PrintCloud prioritizes a **Professional & Clean** aesthetic. The interface uses a minimalist design that maximizes data density and clarity, essential for managing complex ERP data. 
*   **Framework:** Built on Flowbite and Tailwind CSS for a consistent, modern, and responsive experience.
*   **Focus:** Clarity of information and efficiency of navigation.

## Tone & Communication
Communication within the app (tooltips, error messages, notifications) is **Professional, Informative, and Concise**.
*   **Directness:** Guidance is provided clearly and helpfully, without unnecessary fluff.
*   **Efficiency:** Language focuses on the immediate action required, respecting the user's time and expertise.

## Design Systems & Branding
*   **Brand Identity:** PrintCloud uses its established brand colors across the interface to maintain a cohesive identity.
*   **Consistency:** Follows standard web UI patterns to ensure the system is intuitive for all users.

## Data Entry & Workflows
To manage the complexity of print shop operations, PrintCloud employs targeted UI patterns:
*   **Modals & Overlays:** Used for focused tasks and quick data entry, allowing users to remain in their current context.
*   **Multi-Step Wizards:** Complex processes, such as generating detailed job tickets or orders, are broken down into logical, manageable steps to reduce cognitive load and errors.

## Notifications & Real-time Feedback
Users are kept informed through a multi-layered notification strategy:
*   **Toasts:** Non-intrusive, transient alerts for successful actions or minor status updates.
*   **Notification Center:** A centralized dropdown menu for reviewing all recent activities and system alerts.
*   **Real-time Indicators:** Badges and color-coded status changes within tables and dashboards provide immediate visual feedback on production progress.
