# Product Guide - PrintCloud

## Initial Concept
PrintCloud is a modern, full-stack ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system specifically designed for commercial printing presses. It streamlines the entire lifecycle of a print job, from initial customer inquiry and costing to production, delivery, and accounting.

## Target Users
PrintCloud is a comprehensive system catering to all stakeholders in a printing business:
*   **Owners & Managers:** For high-level business insights, financial reporting, and operational oversight.
*   **Sales Representatives:** For managing customer relationships, generating accurate costing sheets, and issuing quotations.
*   **Accountants:** For managing invoices, payments, and overall financial health.
*   **Production Staff:** Including Graphic Designers, Pre-press, Press, and Post-press operators for workflow tracking and job execution.
*   **Storekeepers:** For inventory management of paper, ink, and other consumables.

## Core Value Propositions
*   **Streamlined Operations:** Automating manual processes to reduce errors and save time across all departments.
*   **Business Insights:** Providing real-time reports and data-driven insights to guide business decisions.
*   **Improved Profitability:** Ensuring accurate costing and efficient inventory management to prevent underpricing and waste.
*   **Enhanced Customer Experience:** Faster quote generation and better order tracking for improved client satisfaction.

## Key Modules & Features
*   **Sales & CRM:** Quotation management, Customer database, and a specialized Point of Sale (POS) system for quick print services.
*   **Costing & Estimation:** Advanced calculation grids for precise job estimation.
*   **Production Management:** Job Tickets, Production Scheduling (Pre-press, Press, Post-press), and real-time status tracking.
*   **Financial Management:** Invoicing, Accounting, and financial reporting.
*   **Inventory & Procurement:** Tracking of raw materials and stock levels.

## Technical Vision & Constraints
*   **Modern Stack:** Leveraging Next.js (React) for a responsive frontend and Django (DRF) for a robust backend.
*   **Legacy Integration:** Maintaining connectivity with the legacy MySQL database where necessary during the transition.
*   **Security & Reliability:** Prioritizing data privacy and high system availability for critical business operations.
